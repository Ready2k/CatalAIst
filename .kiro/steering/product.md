# CatalAIst Product Overview

CatalAIst is an AI-powered process classification system that helps organizations identify the best transformation approach for business processes.

## Core Purpose
Classifies business processes into 6 transformation categories:
- **Eliminate** - Remove unnecessary processes
- **Simplify** - Streamline and reduce complexity  
- **Digitise** - Convert manual to digital
- **RPA** - Robotic Process Automation
- **AI Agent** - AI-powered assistance
- **Agentic AI** - Autonomous AI decision-making

## Key Features
- **Intelligent Clarification** - Asks targeted questions to improve accuracy
- **Voice Interface** - Speech-to-text and text-to-speech support (OpenAI & AWS Bedrock)
- **Decision Matrix** - Rule-based logic for consistent classification
- **Learning System** - Improves over time from feedback
- **Blind Evaluation Workflow** - Human-in-the-loop quality control
- **Analytics Dashboard** - Track classification patterns and accuracy
- **Enterprise Security** - JWT auth, rate limiting, PII encryption

## LLM Support
- **OpenAI** - GPT-4, GPT-3.5-turbo, GPT-4o
- **AWS Bedrock** - Claude 3.5 Sonnet, Claude 3 Opus, Claude 3 Haiku, Amazon Nova (Micro, Lite, Pro)

## Target Users
- Business process analysts
- Digital transformation teams
- Operations managers
- IT departments evaluating automation opportunities
